Tranquil Waterfall Sounds




Waterfall Sounds can help you relax, sleep peacefully, block out unwanted noises at home or at work, helps put babies to sleep, aids in studying, meditation, and staying focused.

Waterfall Sounds Fun Fact!




Why does waterfall sounds have so many great benefits? There are many great benefits because a powerful waterfall creates a deep white noise sound. White noise is a random signal having equal intensity at different frequencies, giving it a continual power spectral density, according to Wikipedia.
