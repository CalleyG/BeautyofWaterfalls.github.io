Waterfall Destination Information


The Bond Falls Waterfall is stated to be pet-friendly, has accessible features, roadside parking, an area with picnic tables to grab a bite to eat while visiting the beautiful scenery near the top of the falls, as stated on their website. There is an area for fishing, hiking, cross country skiing, shopping, and even a boardwalk with multiple viewpoints of the waterfall. There is lots to do and everyone has the opportunity to enjoy all that this location has to offer. Grab a friend and head to this beautiful destination for a sense of peace, tranquility, and local fun for Michiganders. For more information, please visit the Bond Falls Scenic Site.


The Niagara Falls Waterfall in Niagara Falls, New York, or Ontario, Canada, will be a great destination to enjoy the beautiful waterfall scenery and its surrounding activities. The website provides information regarding Niagara Falls State Park in New York, which is said to be over 400 acres of protected wildlife and lush landscape, according to their website. The site also states that there are free outdoor concerts and festivals during the summer; events during the fall and winter features food and music. There are also outdoor adventures, shopping, and much more. The main thing is to enjoy the serenity of it all. For more information, please visit theThings to Do in Niagara Falls.


The gorgeous waterfall scene located in Iceland is on an island northwest of the United Kingdom and Norway. There are options such as 182 waterfall tours available to enjoy the waterfall scenery of Iceland. The website has options to book your trip with travel and available dates with pricing which makes life simpler when it comes to serene travel. Great attractions in Iceland such as the Vatnajokull ice caves, Jokulsarlon glacier lagoon, South Coast is described on the site as having beautiful waterfalls, dramatic glaciers, lava fields, and blacksand beaches. Sounds serene? The site even states that there are opportunities to see the Northern Lights and Blue Lagoon. For more information, please visit the Guide To Iceland & Book Your Waterfall Tours.


Featured here is also one of the Secret Waterfalls in Tennessee, located near the base of Signal Mountain. The travel inspiration is from a Tennessee local, Amanda Stravinsky, who gives great detail regarding the mighty cascades located in Tennessee. There are a plethora of waterfall attractions such as the Cane Creek Cascades located in the Falls Creek State Park as stated on the site, Machine Falls which is said to be located in the Short Springs State Natural Area, and Jackson Falls (Duck River) – along the Natchez Trace Parkway driving path. This site houses links to book your trip, events, and much more to get you going on your next calming adventure. For more information, please visit the Secret Waterfalls in Tennessee.
